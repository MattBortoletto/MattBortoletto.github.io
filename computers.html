Computers

Here is a list of computers that I owned in my life:
  1. Some crappy HP Pavilion laptop, 15-inch, Intel Core i3, 8GB RAM, 500GB HDD. This was my personal computer during middle school, mostly used it to play video games. Terrible hardware, terrible Windows OS. At some point it started heating up so much that the plastic on the screen frame started melting.
  2. MacBook Pro (Retina, 13-inch, Early 2015). 2.7GHz dual-core Intel Core i5 processor, 8GB of 1866MHz LPDDR3 onboard memory, Intel Iris Graphics 6100. Shipped with macOS Sierra. This is the computer that I used during my last year of high school, during my BSc and MSc, and even in the first few months of my PhD - 7 years of everyday use. I recently (May 2026) installed Omarchy on it, and I still use it. Probably my favorite computer of all time.
  3. MacBook Pro (13-inch, M1, 2020) with 16GB unified memory, 512GB SSD, and the legendary Backlit Magic Keyboard. This is the computer that I used during my PhD.
  4. MacBook Air (13-inch, M4, 2025) with 16GB unified memory, and 512GB SSD. Beautiful Sky Blue. My private laptop after the 2015 MacBook Pro.
  5. MacBook Pro (16-inch, M5 Max, 2026) with 128GB unified memory and a 2T SSD. My work laptop at kapa.ai. A $8k beast.
